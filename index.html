📖 Our Story

Authentic British experiences since 2012

🇬🇧 Born from passion for British heritage

UK City Tour was founded by a group of historians and local guides who wanted to share the real soul of British cities — beyond postcards. We believe travel should be immersive: from the buzzing football terraces of Manchester to the ancient cobbles of Edinburgh. Every tour is led by storytellers who bring history to life.

⭐ Awarded "Best Cultural Tour Operator 2024" by UK Travel Awards. We’re proud to offer small-group experiences, sustainable practices, and true insider access. Whether you're a history buff, a foodie, or a music lover, we craft journeys that stay with you.

📍 Offices in London, Manchester & Edinburgh. Over 50k happy customers.
